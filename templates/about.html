ABOUT US

Welcome to our website. This is a website designed to solve the problem for the color blind people in their day to day life like differentiating between colors of clothes while shopping or chosing something on the basis of color. The problems faced by them of asking nearby people for help. The sole purpose of this website is to give them freedom from asking "WHAT COLOR IS IT". It is available to them 24x7 and is very convenient to use. We are a team of 5 students aiming to solve the problem of around thirty crore people all around the world. This website is the first step from our end.
